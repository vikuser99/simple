I was first introduced to Japanese karate at a young age. It helped me build confidence and discipline, especially because my sensei was very strict and pushed me to improve.

Currently, I train at [White Dragon](https://www.whitedragonmartialarts.com/), where I am learning kickboxing along with different forms and techniques from the Choi Li Fut style of Kung Fu. Choi Li Fut is a dynamic Southern Chinese martial arts system with a mix of both Northern and Southern techniques.

In addition to striking, I am also learning grappling, which helps me develop a more complete understanding of self-defense and fighting skills.
