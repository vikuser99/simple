I’ve had a few different experiences volunteering through music, basketball, and the Police Cadet Program. Most of it has been about spending time with different groups of people and helping where I can.

With music, I’ve performed at senior homes, schools for the blind, and cultural events. I usually sing Hindustani classical music. These performances were about sharing music with different audiences and seeing how people respond to it in different settings.

With basketball, I volunteered at a youth camp with my varsity team. We worked with kids from around 4th to 8th grade and helped them with basic skills like dribbling, shooting, passing, and defense. We also ran small drills and scrimmages during the week.

Through the Police Cadet Program, I’ve also been part of ride-alongs and community events. It gave me a chance to see how officers interact with the community and what their job looks like day to day.

Overall, volunteering for me has just been about showing up, helping out, and learning from different environments. It’s given me a chance to give back in small ways while doing things I’m already interested in.
